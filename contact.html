❓ FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

💬 Is this website free to use?

Yes! GeoTour is a completely free, static website. There are no subscriptions, fees, or hidden costs.

💬 Can I book trips through GeoTour?

GeoTour is an information platform only. We provide destination data and travel tips, but booking and payments are out of scope for this project.

💬 How accurate are the map locations?

All locations use real-world GPS coordinates and are displayed on Leaflet.js maps using OpenStreetMap data. They are highly accurate.

💬 Can I suggest new destinations?

Absolutely! Use the contact form above to suggest new destinations and we'll review them for inclusion.
